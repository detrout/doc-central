Welcome to Doc-Central

Doc-Central is a tool to conveniently browse the documentation available on your Debian system.

Please, select a category of your interest on the list located on the left side. Once you do that, a list of documents in that category will be shown. Also, if you have a clear idea of what you are looking for, you can look for your specific needs by entering a regular expression and clicking on the 'search' button. If you enter an invalid regular expression, a normal string search will be used instead.

You can view a document by selecting its title. In case it is available in more than one format, all the possibilities will be listed next to the title. Simply choose the format of your choice and it will get displayed.

Enjoy!
