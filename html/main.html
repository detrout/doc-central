Welcome to Doc-Central

Doc-Central is tool to conveniently browse the documentation on your Debian system.

Please select a category of your interest from the list on the left side. Once you select a category a list of documents in that category will appear. You can also search the list of documents by entering a regular expression and clicking on search. If you enter an invalid regular expression a normal string search will be done instead.

You can view a documentation by selecting its title. If it is available in multiple formats the formats will be listed next to the title. Simply selecting the format of your choice will show the document in desired format.
